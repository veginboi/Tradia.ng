1. How do I find sellers near me?
Answer: Tradia uses a digital map showing approved sellers around your location. Verified sellers appear as icons — click one to view their categories and products.
2. Can I trust the sellers on the platform?
Answer: Yes. Every seller goes through registration and approval before appearing on the map. Buyer Protection policies also ensure your transactions are safe.
3. How do I add products to my cart?
Answer: Click a seller → select a category → choose a product → click “Add to Cart.” You can review, edit, or remove items from the cart anytime.
4. Can I buy from multiple sellers at once?
Answer: Yes. Your cart is divided by seller sections, and you can checkout with one seller or all sellers together.
5. How do I track my orders?
Answer: Check your Purchase History to see seller name, amount, date, and order status: Paid → Processing → Delivered.
6. What if I don’t receive my item?
Answer: If your order isn’t delivered within the expected time, you can open a dispute and request a refund through Buyer Protection.
7. How do I access my account settings?
Answer: For security reasons, the settings section can only be accessed through your profile. To open it:
1. Go to your Profile section.
2. Locate the gear/settings icon and click on it.
